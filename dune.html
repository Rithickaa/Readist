Dune

Frank Herbert


Description

“Dune” is an epic science fiction novel set on the desert planet Arrakis, where noble houses battle for control of the planet's precious resource, spice. Paul Atreides, the young heir of House Atreides, is thrust into a deadly conflict and begins a journey of prophecy, politics, and rebellion. The novel explores themes of power, ecology, and destiny in a rich and complex universe.

Meet the author

Frank Herbert was an American science fiction author born in 1920. He is best known for Dune, which is considered one of the greatest science fiction novels ever written. Herbert's work blends philosophy, politics, religion, and environmental themes, setting a high standard for speculative fiction. Dune spawned a series of sequels and inspired generations of sci-fi writers and filmmakers.
