Little Fires Everywhere

Celeste Ng


Description

“Little Fires Everywhere” is a thought-provoking domestic drama set in the idyllic suburb of Shaker Heights, Ohio. The story follows the Richardson family, whose orderly lives are disrupted by the arrival of Mia Warren, an enigmatic artist, and her daughter Pearl. As the town becomes divided over a controversial custody case, secrets unravel, relationships are tested, and the lines between right and wrong blur. This novel explores themes of motherhood, privilege, identity, and the cost of conformity.

Meet the author

Celeste Ng is an acclaimed American author known for her deeply emotional and socially conscious novels. Born in 1980, she grew up in Shaker Heights, which inspired the setting of Little Fires Everywhere. Her works often delve into race, class, and family dynamics, capturing complex human experiences with lyrical prose and sharp insight. Ng’s novels have received critical acclaim and popular success, with Little Fires Everywhere adapted into a popular Hulu series.
