Ender's Game

Orson Scott Card


Description

“Ender’s Game” centers on Ender Wiggin, a gifted child trained at a military space school to defend Earth against alien invaders. As he rises through the ranks, Ender faces brutal tactics, psychological manipulation, and a devastating twist. The novel explores leadership, ethics, and the morality of war.

Meet the author

Orson Scott Card is an American author best known for Ender’s Game, which won both the Hugo and Nebula Awards. Born in 1951, Card is known for combining science fiction with philosophical and moral questions. His writing often explores family, identity, and the complexity of human nature.
