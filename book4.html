Things We Never Got Over

Lucy Score


Description

"Things We Never Got Over" is a contemporary romance novel that follows Naomi Witt, a runaway bride who escapes to the small town of Knockemout, Virginia, only to find herself caring for her estranged twin sister’s daughter. Struggling with sudden responsibility and emotional upheaval, Naomi crosses paths with Knox Morgan, a grumpy yet protective local who prefers solitude. Despite their differences, sparks fly as they confront their pasts and begin to heal. The novel blends humor, heart, and steam, exploring themes of second chances, found family, and unexpected love.

Meet the author

Lucy Score is a bestselling American romance author known for her witty dialogue, strong heroines, and emotionally satisfying love stories. Her books often center around small towns, complex relationships, and the power of personal growth. With a devoted fanbase and a knack for blending humor and heat, Score’s novels appeal to readers who enjoy heartfelt, character-driven romance with happily ever afters.
