Meet the author

Maureen Gibbon is an American novelist and professor whose work often delves into female desire, agency, and transformation. Known for her lyrical and bold prose, Gibbon brings historical figures to life with emotional depth and modern resonance. In addition to Paris Red, she has written other novels and essays that explore complex female characters navigating passion, trauma, and self-discovery.
