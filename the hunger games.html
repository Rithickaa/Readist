The Hunger Games

Suzanne Collins


Description

“The Hunger Games” takes place in a dystopian society where each year, teenagers are chosen to fight to the death in a televised event. Katniss Everdeen volunteers in place of her sister and becomes a reluctant hero. The novel is a fast-paced, emotionally charged story about survival, oppression, and rebellion against a cruel regime.

Meet the author

Suzanne Collins is an American author and screenwriter, born in 1962. She gained global recognition with The Hunger Games trilogy, which became a literary and cinematic phenomenon. Collins blends action, dystopia, and social commentary, creating compelling stories that appeal to both young adult and adult readers.
