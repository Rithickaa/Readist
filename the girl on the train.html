The Girl On The Train

Paula Hawkins


Description

“The Girl on the Train” follows Rachel, a troubled woman who becomes obsessed with a couple she sees every day from her train ride. When the woman goes missing, Rachel inserts herself into the investigation, unraveling secrets and lies that change everything. The novel is a gripping psychological thriller told through multiple unreliable narrators.

Meet the author

Paula Hawkins is a British author born in 1972, best known for her debut thriller The Girl on the Train. Before becoming a novelist, she worked as a journalist. Hawkins is praised for her suspenseful storytelling, deep psychological insights, and her focus on complex female characters.
