It Ends with Us

Colleen Hoover


Description

“The Girl Made of Clay” is a contemporary novel about Sara, a sculptor whose estranged, famous war-photographer father returns into her life after being injured in a fire. As she cares for him, she confronts painful memories and unresolved wounds, all while trying to hold her own family together. The story explores healing, forgiveness, and the complexities of father-daughter relationships.

Meet the author

Nicole Meier is an American author known for her heartfelt contemporary fiction. Her novels often focus on women’s journeys, family dynamics, and emotional growth. With a background in journalism and travel writing, Meier brings rich detail and emotional nuance to her storytelling, making her books resonate with readers who enjoy reflective and moving narratives.
