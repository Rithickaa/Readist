A Court of Thorns and Roses

Sarah J Maas


Description

“A Court of Thorns and Roses” follows Feyre Archeron, a mortal huntress who is taken to the magical land of Prythian as punishment for killing a fae wolf. There, she becomes entangled with Tamlin, a powerful High Fae lord, and discovers an ancient curse threatening their world. As Feyre learns about love, power, and sacrifice, she transforms from a survivor into a heroine in this thrilling romantic fantasy inspired by Beauty and the Beast.

Meet the author

Sarah J. Maas is a #1 New York Times bestselling author known for her fantasy series Throne of Glass, A Court of Thorns and Roses, and Crescent City. Born in 1986 in New York, Maas began writing at a young age and gained a large following through online platforms before publishing her first book. Her stories are known for strong heroines, intricate world-building, and emotionally rich plots that blend romance, action, and magic.
