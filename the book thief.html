The Book Thief

Markus Zusak


Description

“The Book Thief” is a moving historical fiction novel set in Nazi Germany, narrated by Death. It follows Liesel Meminger, a young girl who finds comfort in stealing books and sharing them during a time of war and loss. As she grows up with her foster family and befriends a Jewish man hiding in their basement, she witnesses both the horrors and the beauty of humanity.

Meet the author

Markus Zusak is an Australian author born in 1975. He is best known for The Book Thief, which received international acclaim and numerous awards. His lyrical writing and unique narrative style have earned him praise worldwide. Zusak often explores themes of loss, love, and resilience in his works.
