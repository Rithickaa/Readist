The Silent Patient

Alex Michaelides


Description

“The Silent Patient” is a gripping psychological thriller about Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who shoots her husband and then stops speaking. Theo Faber, a psychotherapist, becomes obsessed with uncovering the truth behind her silence. As he digs deeper into Alicia’s life, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge everything he believes. The novel is known for its shocking twist and complex character study.

Meet the author

Alex Michaelides is a British-Cypriot author and screenwriter. Born in 1977, he studied psychotherapy before turning to writing, which deeply influenced The Silent Patient. The book became a massive international bestseller and was praised for its suspenseful narrative and psychological depth. His storytelling is marked by twist endings, layered characters, and explorations of trauma and mental health.
